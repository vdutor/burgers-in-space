Yangmingshan

A National Park at the edge of Taipei

Posted by Vincent on September 9, 2016

Yangmingshan is a collection of peaks in Taiwan, located to the northeast of Taipei. The park offers great sights on Taipei, raw nature hikes and some traditional hot springs. From NTU a 1.5 hour of metro and bus is needed to get to the park.

Yangminshang is located to the northeast of Taipei. (1.5 hours from NTU)

The park

Hot Springs

Taiwanese people have a rich tradition of bathing in the natural warm water. The water is heated by the lava, which is located underneath the mountains. During our visit we tried the Lengshuiken hot spring. Compared to the other hot springs, Lengshuiken is rather cold. Yet, during our bathing time the water was 41.5 degrees.
